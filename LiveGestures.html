LiveGesture: Streamable Co-Speech Gesture Generation Model

Video Demos

The LiveGesture demo highlights our method for achieving realistic co-speech gesture generation from audio input. Key to our approach is Uncertainty-Guided Sampling (UGS), which iteratively samples high-confidence gesture tokens based on their probabilistic distributions, ensuring natural and expressive gesture sequences. All the demos here are from UGS stage.
